## 9. Statistical Caveats & Limitations

### Sample Size Constraints
- **n=25 observations per stock** (one per year) is statistically small
- Power analysis suggests n=50+ needed for robust single-stock conclusions
- **Aggregate patterns** across 30-80 stocks provide stronger evidence

### Multiple Testing Burden
- **DJIA:** 30 hypotheses tested ‚Üí expect 1.5 false positives (5% FDR)
- **Actual significant:** 5 stocks (17.2%) ‚Üí 3.4x above random chance ‚≠ê
- **NASDAQ-100:** 80 hypotheses tested ‚Üí harder to reach significance after correction

### Survivorship Bias
- **Current constituents only:** Analysis excludes delisted/removed companies
- **Direction of bias:** Positive (surviving companies performed better historically)
- **Magnitude:** Unknown without historical membership data
- **Mitigation:** Focus on relative comparison (both analyses have same bias)

### Market Evolution
- **Adaptive markets hypothesis:** Patterns may diminish as awareness increases
- **Post-publication effect:** Academic anomalies often weaken after discovery
- **Recommendation:** Monitor performance; reduce allocation if edge disappears

## Appendix: Methodology

### Trading Windows Defined

**Thanksgiving Window:**
- **Entry:** 3 business days before Thanksgiving (typically Monday)
- **Exit:** 1 business day after Thanksgiving (Black Friday)
- **Duration:** 4 business days
- **Special:** Black Friday is half-day session (closes 1:00 PM ET)

**Cyber Monday Window:**
- **Entry:** 3 business days before Cyber Monday (typically prior Wednesday)
- **Exit:** Cyber Monday (typically Monday after Thanksgiving)
- **Duration:** 4 business days
- **Special:** Full trading sessions (no early close)

### Statistical Methods

**Return Calculation:**
```
Simple Return = (Close_price - Open_price) / Open_price
```

**Hypothesis Test:**
- **Null hypothesis (H‚ÇÄ):** Median return = 0 (no seasonality effect)
- **Alternative (H‚ÇÅ):** Median return ‚â† 0 (seasonality exists)
- **Test statistic:** Wilcoxon signed-rank test (non-parametric)
- **Correction:** Benjamini-Hochberg FDR procedure (Œ±=0.05)
- **Significance:** Reject H‚ÇÄ if p_corrected < 0.05

**Multiple Testing Correction:**
```
Benjamini-Hochberg Procedure:
1. Sort p-values in ascending order: p‚ÇÅ ‚â§ p‚ÇÇ ‚â§ ... ‚â§ p‚Çô
2. For each test i, compute threshold: (i/n) √ó Œ±
3. Find largest i where p·µ¢ ‚â§ (i/n) √ó Œ±
4. Reject H‚ÇÄ for all tests 1 through i
```

### Data Processing

**Trading Calendar:**
- **Source:** NYSE calendar with 10 federal holidays
- **Holidays:** New Year's Day, MLK Day, Presidents' Day, Good Friday, Memorial Day, Independence Day, Labor Day, Thanksgiving, Christmas
- **Business day shifting:** If holiday falls on weekend, shift forward/backward per NYSE rules
